# Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment

## Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze how market sentiment influences cryptocurrency trader performance using the Fear & Greed Index and historical Hyperliquid trading data.

## Dataset

- Fear & Greed Index
- Historical Trading Dataset

## Data Preparation

- Removed unmatched records
- Converted timestamps
- Merged datasets by date
- No missing values after preprocessing

## Key Findings

- Extreme Greed produced the highest average PnL (67.89).
- Extreme Greed achieved the highest win rate (46.49%).
- Extreme Fear showed the weakest trading performance.
- Larger trade size alone did not guarantee higher profitability.

## Recommendations

- Increase exposure cautiously during Extreme Greed.
- Reduce position sizes during Extreme Fear.
- Maintain disciplined risk management regardless of market sentiment.

## Conclusion

Market sentiment significantly influences trading behavior. Incorporating sentiment indicators into trading strategies can improve decision-making and risk management.
